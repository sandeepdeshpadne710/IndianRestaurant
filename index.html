India Restaurant is a trendy dining restaurant serving modern-Indian cuisine, first of its kind in India, served exclusively by speech & hearing-impaired service staff. The restaurant offers seasonal and select classical dishes from select locations across India with contemporary expression presented by a highly experienced kitchen team. Good food served quietly.
